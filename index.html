Vocabulize

Vocabuilze is a visual learning aid and front end application that allows users to utilize their visual memory and artistic side with memory and recognition. This project was created in collaboration with Erika Manning for the Mintbean Jr. Developer Hackathon in November of 2021.

PetSense

PetSense is an application and wearable device for your pet that brings their health data and well being straight to your fingertips and desktop. It is currently in the regsearch, design, and development phase.

The National Aquarium

In 2018 the National Aquarium in Baltimore Maryland has a strange issue: birds kept flying into the glass to meet their demise. In this project, constructed at Applied Image we increased the visibility of the glass by creating a decorative and functional pattern.
